Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency

  • Kicking the Hornet's Nest: "Kicking the Hornet's Nest," edition 3, is now live with 100+ new "Satoshi emails." The Complete Writings, Emails, and Forum Posts of Satoshi Nakamoto, the Founder of Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency. Available in print from Lulu (preferred seller) or Amazon, or free digitally. Featured on Bitcoin.com in a book review.
  • Satoshi Bitcoin Converter: App to check and convert bitcoin/altcoin with fiat prices at SatoshiBitcoinConverter.com. Started in 2013, one of my first crypto projects.
  • ecash by sound: ecash can be sent digitally, visually via QR code, or on paper. How about audibly? On June 20, 2024, 21 sats were successfully sent as a cashu/ecash token.
  • Ordinals: Everything Satoshi wrote is on-chain as ordinal inscriptions. The words that started Bitcoin now live on the blockchain they created. Links and info at peakd.com/bitcoin/@crrdlx/satoshi.
  • The Bitkenstan: A web or NFT comic about Bitcoin, crypto, blockchain, and life at bitkenstan.vercel.app and hive.blog/@bitkenstan.
  • The Hodl Song: A silly sea shanty song, almost as good as the Christmas Puppy song. Other music available.
  • Bitcointalk.org: My few posts on the original Bitcoin forum set up by Satoshi, starting in June 2012.

Nostr
