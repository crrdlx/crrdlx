Introduction

👋 I'm crrdlx, call me CR for short. I like bitcoin, nostr, blockchain, and I do some creative projects, fiddling and learning. You can see things I've done and contact info at hive.blog/@crrdlx/about. Most notable projects: (a) "Kicking the Hornet's Nest" and (b) "Satoshi Bitcoin Converter".

Bitcoin

These are things I've done that are bitcoin-related:

  • Kicking the Hornet's Nest - The Complete Writings, Emails, and Forum Posts of Satoshi Nakamoto, the Founder of Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency can be downloaded for free or purchased as a real book. Edition 1 of the book was featured on Bitcoin.com in this book review. Edition 3, is now live with 100+ new "Satoshi emails." Available in print from Lulu (preferred seller) or Amazon, or free digitally.
  • Satoshi Bitcoin Converter - "SBC" is a simple PWA to check and convert bitcoin/altcoin with fiat prices - SatoshiBitcoinConverter.com - Started in 2013, it was my first bitcoin project. So, it's dear to my 🧡
  • Golden Digital - "Golden Digital" is a simple PWA to check and convert gold/silver with fiat prices - GoldenDigital.vercel.app
  • ecash by sound - ecash can be sent digitally, visually via a QR code reader, on paper. How about audibly? Can ecash be sent via sound? On June 20, 2024, 21 sats were successfully sent as a ecash/cashu token. 🙂
  • Ordinals - I put edition 1 of "Kicking" on the Bitcoin chain as ordinal inscriptions, see inscription 34145265. The words that started Bitcoin and cryptocurrency now are actually live on the blockchain that they created. They're not reliant on a server, somewhere, run by someone...Satoshi's words are actually hosted on Bitcoin itself. Links and info at hive.blog/bitcoin/@crrdlx/satoshi
  • BlockRoss: Uses the Bitcoin blockchain to generate random art at blockross.vercel.app. A tip o' the cap to Bob Ross.
  • Bitcointalk.org - See my very few posts on the original Bitcoin forum set up by Satoshi himself. Looks like I started posting in June of 2012.

Nostr

My nostr-related projects:

  • My nostr profile: Nostr isn't Bitcoin, but many bitcoiners are there, it's decentralized, and zapping sats makes it feel close. See my profile and npub at https://njump.me/npub1qpd...082j.
  • Nostr Wiki: A wiki for Nostr at nostrwiki.vercel.app. Navigate the sidebar and edit or add content.
  • Hostr - Hostr is a bidirectional bridge between the Hive blockchain and Nostr. What you post on one is automatically cross-posted to the other. See GitHub repo: https://github.com/crrdlx/hostr
  • Nostrdon: A unidirectional bridge from Nostr to Mastodon. Posts on Nostr are cross-posted to Mastodon. See GitHub repo: github.com/crrdlx/nostrdon.

Hive

I like the Hive blockchain:
